## ğŸ¯ Overview

**Gilded Edge Bistro** is a sophisticated restaurant website designed to provide an exceptional digital experience. The site showcases our commitment to gourmet dining, sustainable sourcing, and impeccable service through an elegant, modern interface.

Founded in 2026, Gilded Edge Bistro combines traditional culinary excellence with contemporary techniques, sourcing 90% of ingredients from local farms.

## âœ¨ Features

### Visual Design
- **Elegant & Modern Interface** - Clean, sophisticated design with premium aesthetics
- **Responsive Layout** - Fully responsive design that works seamlessly on mobile, tablet, and desktop
- **Smooth Animations** - Fade-in effects, hover states, and scroll animations for engaging user experience
- **Premium Color Scheme** - Carefully chosen bistro-inspired colors (gold, green, dark brown)

### Interactive Elements
- **Animated Navigation** - Underline hover effects on nav links with active page indicators
- **Card Hover Effects** - Lift and shadow animations on menu items and content cards
- **Form Interactions** - Smooth focus states and scaling effects on form inputs
- **Icon Animations** - Scaling and color transitions on interactive elements

### Content Sections
- **Home Page** - Hero section with call-to-action buttons, feature highlights, and reservation prompt
- **Menu Page** - Comprehensive menu with 5 categories:  Starters, Main Courses, Pasta & Grains, Sides, and Beverages
- **About Page** - Brand story, culinary vision, sourcing philosophy, and team information
- **Contact Page** - Contact form, location details, phone, email, and business hours
